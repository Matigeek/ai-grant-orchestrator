# Example Use Case

## 🧪 Medical University of Lodz (UMED)

AI Grant Orchestrator can support university-level innovation and grant offices in improving research funding performance.

---

## 🎯 Problem

- Researchers struggle to identify relevant funding opportunities  
- Grant support teams operate reactively  
- Limited scalability of personalized support  

---

## 🚀 Solution

AI Grant Orchestrator enables:

- automated matching of researchers with funding calls  
- AI-generated career development pathways  
- structured analysis of funding opportunities  
- support for early-stage idea validation  

---

## ⚙️ Example Workflow

1. Researcher submits profile (field, experience, goals)  
2. System matches relevant funding calls  
3. AI generates:
   - funding strategy  
   - career roadmap  
   - proposal positioning suggestions  

---

## 🌍 Expected Impact

- increased number of submitted proposals  
- higher success rates in competitive calls  
- better alignment between research and funding priorities  
- scalable support for large research communities  

---

## 🔮 Future Development

- integration with institutional databases  
- real-time funding call updates  
- dashboard for research managers  
- collaboration recommendations (consortium building)
