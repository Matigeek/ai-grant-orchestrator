# Prompts

Collection of example prompts used in AI Grant Orchestrator.

---

## 🔍 Grant Matching

Based on this researcher profile:

- Field: [e.g. digital health]
- Career stage: [PhD / postdoc / senior]
- Publications: [short summary]

Suggest:
- relevant Horizon Europe, EIT Health, and Erasmus+ calls
- justification for each match
- probability of success

---

## 🧭 Career Path Planning

Create a 3–5 year development plan for a researcher aiming to secure international funding.

Include:
- recommended funding programs
- skill gaps to fill
- suggested collaborations
- milestones and timeline

---

## 📊 Call Analysis

Analyze the following funding call:

[PASTE CALL TEXT]

Extract:
- key objectives
- evaluation criteria
- expected outcomes
- success factors

Given this research idea:

[DESCRIPTION]
