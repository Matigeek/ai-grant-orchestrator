# Funding Database (Starter)

Curated list of international funding opportunities relevant for research, innovation, and academic ecosystems.

---

## 🇪🇺 Horizon Europe

### HORIZON-EIE (Innovation Ecosystems)
- Focus: innovation ecosystems, startup support, university-industry collaboration
- Target: universities, startups, innovation hubs
- Example topics:
  - startup acceleration
  - knowledge valorisation
  - ecosystem building

---

### WIDERA (Widening Participation)
- Focus: strengthening research capacity in widening countries
- Target: universities in Central & Eastern Europe
- Instruments:
  - ERA Chairs
  - Twinning
  - Excellence Hubs

---

### MSCA Postdoctoral Fellowships
- Focus: researcher mobility and career development
- Target: postdocs
- Key feature: international mobility + training

---

## 🧠 EIT (European Institute of Innovation & Technology)

### EIT Health – Innovation Calls
- Focus: healthcare innovation, validation, commercialization
- Target: startups, universities, hospitals
- Example:
  - Innovation Validation Call
  - Flagship programmes

---

### EIT Health – Education
- Focus: training, upskilling, deep tech talent
- Target: universities, training providers
- Example:
  - Deep Tech Talent Initiative
  - Labelled programmes

---

## 🌍 Erasmus+

### Erasmus+ KA220 (Cooperation Partnerships)
- Focus: education innovation, collaboration
- Target: universities, NGOs, schools
- Example:
  - adult education
  - digital education
  - health education

---

### Erasmus+ KA210 (Small-scale partnerships)
- Focus: smaller collaborative projects
- Good for: early-stage consortia

---

## 🌐 Interreg

### Interreg Central Europe
- Focus: regional innovation, digital health, sustainability
- Target: public institutions, universities, regions
- Example:
  - digital health pilots
  - value-based healthcare

---

## 🇪🇺 Other

### COST Actions

### IHI (Innovative Health Initiative)

- Target: academia + industry consortia
